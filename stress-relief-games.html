Stress Relief Games

Take a break and enjoy our collection of relaxing games. Choose your favorite and let the stress melt away.

Count Masters
~10 min

Count Masters

A fun stickman running game with crowd mechanics

How to Play

Swipe left or right to move your crowd. Collect more stickmen by running through number gates. Avoid obstacles and defeat enemies by having more stickmen in your crowd. Perfect for quick stress relief sessions.

Solar Smash
~20 min

Solar Smash

Explore and destroy planets in this relaxing sandbox game

How to Play

Select different weapons and tools from the menu and tap on the planet to use them. Experiment with different combinations to create unique destruction patterns. A perfect way to release tension through creative destruction.

Bridge Race
~15 min

Bridge Race

Build bridges and race to the finish line

How to Play

Collect blocks of your color to build bridges and advance to higher levels. Avoid opponents and try to reach the top first. Use strategy to block opponents while efficiently collecting your own blocks. Great for quick mental refreshment.

MX Offroad Master
~25 min

MX Offroad Master

Master challenging offroad tracks with your motorcycle

How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to control your motorcycle. Balance is key - use Up/W to accelerate, Down/S to brake, and Left/Right (A/D) to adjust your weight. Complete challenging tracks while maintaining balance. Perfect for focusing your mind away from stress.

Traffic Rider
~15 min

Traffic Rider

An exciting motorcycle racing game through traffic

How to Play

Control your motorcycle with arrow keys or WASD. Navigate through traffic at high speeds, complete missions and earn points by near-misses. Perfect for adrenaline rush and focusing your mind away from everyday stress.

Flappy Bird
~3 min

Flappy Bird

The classic bird-flapping game that tests your reflexes

How to Play

Tap the screen or press spacebar to make the bird flap its wings and stay airborne. Navigate through the pipes without touching them. Simple yet challenging, this game helps you focus entirely on the present moment - perfect for mindfulness practice.
